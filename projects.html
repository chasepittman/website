The Idea

Zoom Office is a concept product designed to bridge the gap between remote and in-person work environments.

The idea was to create a virtual workplace inside Zoom that recreates the spatial awareness and casual collaboration of a real office.

Problem
  • Remote work can feel isolating
  • Hybrid teams struggle with visibility
  • Meeting culture creates fatigue
Solution
  • Virtual office floor plans
  • Real-time employee presence
  • Interactive meeting rooms
  • Department filtering
  • Integrated scheduling
Key Features
  • Click rooms to join meetings
  • See who is in the office
  • Virtual task boards
  • Break rooms and recharge spaces
  • VR support for immersive workspaces
Measuring Impact
  • Employee engagement surveys
  • Application usage time
  • Turnover rate
  • Productivity impact
