The Idea

Zoom Office is a concept product designed to recreate the spatial awareness and collaboration of a physical office within Zoom.

Problem
  • Remote work reduces spontaneous collaboration
  • Zoom fatigue from meeting-heavy culture
  • Lack of visibility across hybrid teams
Solution
  • Interactive office floorplans
  • Real-time employee presence
  • Clickable meeting rooms
  • Department filtering
Key Features
  • Virtual whiteboards
  • Task boards
  • Break rooms
  • VR collaboration support
Measuring Impact
  • Employee satisfaction surveys
  • Usage time
  • Employee retention
  • Productivity improvement
