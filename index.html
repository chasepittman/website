My Learnings

  • 🚀
    Just ship it. Build your highest impact features first, and tweak later. Don't get stuck in the details, refining endlessly before you've put out a finished product. Post-launch iteration beats delayed perfection.
  • 🧱
    Build it yourself. Starting with a blank file challenged me to understand the fundamentals and end-to-end process of building a product in a way no template ever could have.
  • 🔍
    Always come back to why. As you build, it's important to think of every element through the eyes of your "customer" and ensure you have a reason behind every feature. Data to support is always a plus.
